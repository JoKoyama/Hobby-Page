Introduction

A lock is usually any device that is mechanical or electrical that protects something on the other side of the lock. They can usually be opened by a key or code and but some can be disabled or broken by force. In the past locks were big, expensive, and somewhat easy to open due to it's size and simple design. For example this wooden deadlock is one of the first locks to be used. The problem was it could be unlocked from the inside and it can only be used when you are inside the door. In the present locks are smaller and have more combination possibilities to stop locks from being opened on accident. This lock has 64000 combonations and is made of metal increasing it's resistance to the lock being opened by force. Finally there are electric lock, which use a variety of fingerprint retina scan or combonation. These locks are the most secure but come with a very fatal weakness.
